About Us

Welcome to CourseHub 🎓 – a modern online course discovery platform designed to help learners explore high-quality free courses and earn valuable certificates from trusted global platforms.

Our mission is to make learning accessible for everyone by connecting students with top educational resources such as freeCodeCamp, Saylor Academy, HP LIFE, Cisco Networking Academy, and more. Whether you are a beginner or looking to upgrade your skills, CourseHub helps you find the right path.

At CourseHub, we believe education should be simple, free, and accessible. That’s why we bring curated learning opportunities in one place so you can focus on learning instead of searching.

Users can explore various categories, discover new skills, and directly visit official platforms to start learning and earn certificates at zero cost.

This platform is built using Python (Django) with a responsive frontend and full backend integration, developed as part of an academic project to demonstrate real-world web development skills.
