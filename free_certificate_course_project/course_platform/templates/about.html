About Us

Welcome to CourseHub 🎓 – a platform designed to help students find free online courses with certificates from trusted learning platforms.

Our website provides information about various platforms such as freeCodeCamp, Saylor Academy, HP LIFE, and Cisco Networking Academy where students can learn new skills and earn certificates.

Users can explore different courses, visit the official platforms, and improve their knowledge without any cost.

This project is developed using Python (Django) with proper frontend and backend integration as part of an academic lab project.
